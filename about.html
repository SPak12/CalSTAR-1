We are UC Berkeley's FIRST high-powered rocketry team . Founded in Fall 2015 , CalSTAR (a.k.a. Cal Space Technologies and Rocketry Team) is an emerging team looking to compete in NASA's Student Launch Initiative . Our beginning plans consist of team growth and obtaining certification for launching rockets. By the end of 2016, we hope to be on our way to launching our very own rocket. As the team grows and establishes itself, we hope to do research in the fields of space, technology, and rocketry. We would also like to reach out to the community of Berkeley and the Bay Area to establish an aerospace community that doesn't exist currently. This is just the beginnng!
