We are UC Berkeley's FIRST high-powered rocketry team. Founded in Fall 2015, CalSTAR, a.k.a. Cal Space Technolgoies and Rocketry Team, is a brand new team looking to compete in NASA's Student Launch Initiative. Our beginning plans consist of increasing the size of our team and getting certification for launching rockets. By the end of 2016, we hope to be on our way to launching our very own rocket. As the team grows and establishes itself, we hope to do research in the fields of space/rockets and outreach to the community of Berkeley and the Bay Area. This is just the beginnng!
